🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 May 2026 · Effective: Tanzania Personal Data Protection Act, 2022

Nodalali ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account info: Email, password (hashed), phone number
  • Listings: Property details you post (title, location, price, photos, contact)
  • Usage: Pages viewed, listings favorited, IP address, device type
  • Payments: M-Pesa transaction IDs (we do NOT store your PIN)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To show your listings to other users
  • To process payments via ZenoPay (M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money)
  • To send SMS verification codes via Beem Africa
  • To detect fraud and enforce our rules
  • To send you transactional emails (new inquiries, payment confirmations)

3. Data Sharing

We share data with:

  • ZenoPay — for payment processing
  • Beem Africa — for SMS delivery
  • Brevo — for email delivery
  • Firebase (Google) — for hosting and authentication
  • Tanzania authorities — when legally required

We do NOT sell your data to third parties.

4. Your Rights (TZ Personal Data Protection Act)

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to delete your account and data
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with the Personal Data Protection Commission

5. Data Retention

We keep your account data while your account is active. Listings are deleted 90 days after expiry. Reports and audit logs are kept 2 years for fraud prevention.

6. Security

We use HTTPS, password hashing (bcrypt), rate limiting, scam keyword filtering, and image moderation. However, no system is 100% secure.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising.

8. Children

Nodalali is not intended for users under 18.
